Presenting Vaishali S Couture at FDCI X Lakmé Fashion Week

Vaishali S Couture’s collection, ""Fil Rouge” followed through the reassessment of key pillars which also define the arc of the designers creative journey. The collection composed of textures and state-of-the-art silhouettes mostly speaking through layers and mixes of drapes and constructions inspired from different weaves of India. Subtle hues all the way from black to colors convey festivity but also tranquility and balance. Sustainability being the strong focus and with the aim of making it transparent also to the customers, the designer ,through her pieces, has traced all processes through Blockchain in order to make it clear and certain.
