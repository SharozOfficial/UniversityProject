Maybelline stands against hate, discrimination and racism in any form. We stand in solidarity with the Asian community. There is no tolerance for the recurring violence towards the Asian and Pacific Islander community. We will be donating to Stop AAPI Hate. @stopaapihate provides multilingual resources to report acts of violence, safety tips, and up-to-date reports on the safety of your area. Visit stopaapihate.org to learn more and check out our stories for additional mental health resources. ❤️ #stopasianhate
