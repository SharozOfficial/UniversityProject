Watch to see how @qualityglowup achieves the Perfect brows in 5 easy steps using our Total Temptation Brow Pencil and #instantagerewind concealer. 

1. Brush brow hairs upwards.
2. Outline the bottom front half of the brow.
3. Brush hair downwards.
4. Outline the top of the brow. Tip: Avoid the very front for a natural look.
5. Fill in the brow as dark as you desire.
Optional step: clean edges with concealer for a more defined look.
