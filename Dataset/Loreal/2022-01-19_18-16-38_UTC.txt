[BLONDE HAIR NEUTRALIZATION]
🇬🇧 We know you love hair transformations!💥
To achieve the best result on your clients’ blonde hair, make sure to follow the below process: 
1⃣ Apply evenly Chroma Crème purple shampoo on wet hair. 🟣 
2⃣ Emulsify.
3⃣ Leave on time: 2 to 5 minutes.
4⃣ Rinse thoroughly until all dyes disappear.
