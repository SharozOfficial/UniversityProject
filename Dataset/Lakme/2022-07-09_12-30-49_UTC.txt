Presenting NBA 75 X Ananya Modi Jain at FDCI X Lakmé Fashion Week 

NBA 75 X Ananya Modi Jain’s new collection redefined basketball culture with a pinch of sass. It centred on vibrant and audacious embellishments inspired by the court’s floor plan, the net, the New York skyline, the iconic teams and the NBA logo. This form-and-function driven all-weather collection is a celebration of the 75 years of NBA. The relaxed silhouettes and versatile cuts make them a wardrobe staple and perfect conversation starters - reflecting fresh twist on the classics.
